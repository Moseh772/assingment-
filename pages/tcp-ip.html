TCP/IP

Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol is the fundamental suite of communication protocols used to interconnect network devices on the Internet. TCP breaks data into packets and ensures reliable delivery, while IP handles addressing and routing. It is the backbone of modern networking.
